About

It's Time to Double Down! The commercial grade carpet tile from Elekto Design that is perfect for any business setting. Made with level loop construction and a PVC backing, these tiles are designed to withstand heavy foot traffic and last for years to come.

Our 100% permacolor polypropylene face fibers are solution dyed, ensuring that the vibrant color of your Double Down tiles will never fade. With a 1/10 gauge and a 5mm thickness, these tiles are both durable and comfortable underfoot.

Each tile measures 19.7 inches by 19.7 inches (500mm x 500mm) and features a unique pattern of parallel lines in alternating widths, which creates a striking gradient, adding a touch of modern style and elegance to your space. With a tufted weight of 26 ounces, these tiles are sure to hold up to even the toughest conditions.

Double Down is available in two styles to choose from, so you can find the perfect match for your business. Desire an even more striking look? Mix and match Double Down styles to create stunning designs! Or you can mix and match with our other Carpet Tile lines; Profusion and Lucky Break to create truly unique spaces. And with a 10 year limited commercial warranty, you can rest assured that your investment is protected.

Upgrade your business space with Double Down from Elekto Design. Contact us today to learn more and place your order.
